Cybersecurity Tips

PROTECT YOURSELF
🔐
Use Strong, Unique Passwords
Use at least 12 characters combining letters, numbers, and symbols. Never reuse passwords across sites. Use a password manager like Bitwarden (free).
📱
Enable 2-Factor Authentication
Turn on 2FA on all your accounts — M-PESA, email, social media. Even if someone steals your password, they can't log in without your phone.
🔗
Verify Links Before Clicking
Hover over links before clicking. Watch for typos like "safaricom.co.ke" vs "safaricorn.co.ke". Scammers create fake sites that look identical to real ones.
💰
Never Pay to Get a Job
Legitimate employers never ask you to pay registration fees, training fees, or processing fees. If a job requires payment upfront — it is a scam.
🌐
Use a VPN on Public Wi-Fi
Avoid accessing your bank or M-PESA on public Wi-Fi at cafes or airports. Hackers can intercept your data. Use a VPN like ProtonVPN (free tier available).
📧
Recognise Phishing Emails
Banks and M-PESA will NEVER ask for your PIN or password via email or phone. Urgent, threatening language like "your account will be closed" is a red flag.
🔑
Protect Your Crypto Seed Phrase
Your crypto seed phrase (12-24 words) is like a master key. Never type it online, share it with anyone, or store it in a photo on your phone.
📞
Verify Caller Identity
If someone calls claiming to be from your bank, Safaricom, or DCI — hang up and call the official number back yourself. Scammers can spoof any caller ID.
