COMMON QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know

Is the free plan really free forever?

Yes, completely. No credit card needed, no hidden charges. The free plan gives you weekly scam alert emails and access to our public scam database with no expiry date.

How do I pay for Premium?

Premium is KSh 99/month paid securely via M-PESA, debit card, or bank transfer through Paystack — Kenya's most trusted payment platform.

What is the Personal Pro plan exactly?

You get direct WhatsApp access to Samuel Adikah, our founder and security analyst. Every month he reviews your accounts and devices for vulnerabilities, and you can message him anytime to verify if something looks like a scam.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime with no penalties. If you cancel Premium, you automatically drop to the free plan and continue receiving weekly email alerts.

Who is the Business plan for?

SACCOs, schools, churches, NGOs, and small businesses in Kenya. If your organisation handles money or personal data, your staff need cybersecurity awareness. We cover up to 10 people for KSh 2,000/month.

How quickly do alerts arrive?

Free subscribers receive alerts within hours of publication. Premium subscribers receive email alerts instantly AND an SMS within seconds of publication — before the alert is even visible on the website.
